Who Are We?

UBC Rover is a multidisciplinary team of 64 students at the University of British Columbia dedicated to designing and building the next generation of semi-autonomous Mars rovers. Competing in the University Rover Challenge, we push the boundaries of robotics through six specialized sub-teams: Chassis, Arm, Software, Science, Electrical, and Business. From machine-learning-driven navigation to innovative swappable end-effectors, we combine technical excellence with community outreach to inspire the next wave of STEM leaders.

Since our inception, we have strived to push the boundaries of student robotics, earning top placements at the University Rover Challenge (URC) and the Canadian International Rover Challenge (CIRC).
